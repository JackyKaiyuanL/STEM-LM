WorldClim v2.1 Bioclimatic Variables
======================================
Source:   https://worldclim.org/data/worldclim21.html
Version:  2.1 (released 2020)
License:  CC BY 4.0
Citation: Fick & Hijmans (2017), Int. J. Climatol. 37:4302–4315

Variables (19 total → env_00 to env_18):
  env_00  bio1   Annual Mean Temperature (°C × 10)
  env_01  bio2   Mean Diurnal Range (Mean of monthly (max − min temp))
  env_02  bio3   Isothermality (bio2/bio7 × 100)
  env_03  bio4   Temperature Seasonality (std dev × 100)
  env_04  bio5   Max Temperature of Warmest Month
  env_05  bio6   Min Temperature of Coldest Month
  env_06  bio7   Temperature Annual Range (bio5 − bio6)
  env_07  bio8   Mean Temperature of Wettest Quarter
  env_08  bio9   Mean Temperature of Driest Quarter
  env_09  bio10  Mean Temperature of Warmest Quarter
  env_10  bio11  Mean Temperature of Coldest Quarter
  env_11  bio12  Annual Precipitation (mm)
  env_12  bio13  Precipitation of Wettest Month
  env_13  bio14  Precipitation of Driest Month
  env_14  bio15  Precipitation Seasonality (CV)
  env_15  bio16  Precipitation of Wettest Quarter
  env_16  bio17  Precipitation of Driest Quarter
  env_17  bio18  Precipitation of Warmest Quarter
  env_18  bio19  Precipitation of Coldest Quarter

Spatial resolution:  30 arc-seconds (~1 km at equator)
Temporal basis:      1970–2000 average (climatological normals)
Projection:          WGS84 geographic (EPSG:4326)
Format:              GeoTIFF, one file per variable

DOWNLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Option A — full global tiles (simplest, ~1.5 GB):
  https://worldclim.org/data/worldclim21.html
  → "Bioclimatic variables" → "30s" resolution
  Downloads: wc2.1_30s_bio.zip
  Unzip into raw/

Option B — regional tiles (smaller download):
  Use WorldClim tile downloads for specific regions.
  Tiles available at 2.5 min, 5 min, 10 min resolution if 30s is too large.

After download, merge into a single multiband GeoTIFF in order bio1..bio19:
  gdal_merge.py -separate -o worldclim_19band.tif \
    raw/wc2.1_30s_bio_1.tif raw/wc2.1_30s_bio_2.tif ... raw/wc2.1_30s_bio_19.tif

PROCESSING NOTES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

- No reprojection needed (already WGS84 geographic).
- NoData values: use bilinear interpolation when resampling to match
  SoilGrids/DEM resolution if needed; otherwise extract at native 1 km.
- For point extraction (plot coordinates): use rasterio sample() at
  exact lat/lon — no spatial averaging needed at plot scale.
- For inference grids at 0.1°: extract at grid centroid coordinates.
